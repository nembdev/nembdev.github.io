## Infrastructure as Code - What Is It?
The answer to managing large-scale, distributed systems, cloud-native applications, and service-based architectures.

The focus is now on API driven cloud environments.

The Resource Life-cycle has changed.
* Months to Years -> Days to Weeks

Scale has grown massively
* Few large instances -> Many Small Instances

There's a growing  demand for Cyclical and Elastic capabilities.

Manual Operation can often times be impossible.

An automated, repeatable, codified, and trackable way of operation is the NOW.
