# Terraform X Booking.com
[Using Terraform Enterprise to Support 3000 Users at Booking.com](https://www.hashicorp.com/resources/using-terraform-enterprise-to-support-3000-users-at-booking-com)

## Key Takeaways

* Learn packer for building image pipelines
* Don't get to a point where things are "constantly breaking — and consistently breaking" LOL :)
* Ownership and tracking is should be a priority from jump
* Real world environments come with contstraints
* Policy as Code tools, such as Sentinel, can help mitigate future disasters
* Add support for "Advanced Devs" from the start
* Repos for seperation and Terraform Scaling
